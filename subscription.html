— FAQ

Common questions

How does the subscription work?

Sign up, take a short wellness quiz, and we'll deliver a curated box of supplements to your door each month. Bills automatically. Skip, pause, or cancel anytime — no commitment.

Can I customise what's in my box?

Yes! After your quiz, you'll see your recommended box. You can swap any product for another from our catalogue before each shipment.

When will I be charged?

Your first charge is on signup. After that, you'll be billed on the same date each month, 3 days before your box ships.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. Cancel anytime from your account dashboard — no questions, no fees. We want you to feel good about the experience.

What's the return policy?

Not happy with your box? Email us within 30 days and we'll refund you in full, no questions asked. Our 30-day happiness guarantee.

Do you ship outside Malaysia?

Currently we only ship within Malaysia. International shipping is launching in late 2026 — sign up to the newsletter to be notified.
