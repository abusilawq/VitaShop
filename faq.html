Orders & Shipping

How long does shipping take in Malaysia?

Klang Valley orders are dispatched same-day if placed before 2pm and typically arrive within 1–2 business days. Outside Klang Valley, expect 2–4 business days. East Malaysia (Sabah/Sarawak) takes 3–5 business days. We use J&T, Poslaju, and DHL eCommerce for delivery.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! Free shipping on all orders over RM150 across Malaysia. Wellness Box subscribers also enjoy free shipping every month, regardless of order value.

Can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email and SMS. You can also view all your order statuses and tracking info from your Account dashboard.

Products & Quality

Are your supplements halal certified?

Most of our supplements are JAKIM-certified halal. Look for the halal badge on individual product pages. Our gelatin capsules use plant-based or bovine-source ingredients that are halal-compliant. We do not sell anything containing pork-derived gelatin or alcohol.

How do I know your products are authentic?

Every batch is third-party lab tested for purity, potency, and contaminants. We're registered with the Malaysian Ministry of Health (KKM) and source directly from approved manufacturers. Each product carries a Certificate of Analysis (COA) available on request.

Are your products suitable for vegans?

Many of our products are 100% plant-based and vegan-friendly. Look for the "Vegan" tag on product pages. Our plant-based protein, multivitamin, omega-3 (algae-derived option), and herbal supplements are all suitable for vegans.

Payments & Refunds

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept FPX (Maybank2u, CIMB Clicks, Public Bank, RHB, Hong Leong, AmBank, Bank Islam, BSN), GrabPay, Touch 'n Go eWallet, Boost, and Visa/Mastercard/AMEX credit and debit cards. All transactions are processed via PCI-compliant gateways with 256-bit SSL encryption.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with your purchase, contact us within 30 days for a full refund or exchange. Products must be unopened and in original packaging. For opened supplements, we'll review on a case-by-case basis.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We never store your full credit card details on our servers. All payments are processed through PCI-DSS Level 1 compliant gateways (iPay88, Stripe). The entire site uses TLS 1.3 encryption (the green padlock you see in your browser).

Wellness Box Subscription

How does the Wellness Box work?

You complete a quick wellness quiz, our nutritionists curate 3–4 supplements tailored to your goals, and we ship the box to your door every month. You save 25% vs. buying individually, plus get free shipping and members-only access to new products.

Can I skip or cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes — no commitments. You can skip any month, change your products, or cancel entirely from your account dashboard. Just do it at least 3 days before your next shipment date.

Can I customise what's in my box?

Yes! After your first box, you can swap any product, add extras, or build a fully custom selection. Our nutritionists also offer free 15-minute consultations to help you fine-tune your routine.
